about

Hi and welcome to my website!

I am a Researcher at Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna, Institute of Economics, working on the SCI-PET project: Socioeconomic and Climate Inequality: emissions, impacts and perceptions to design Policy for an Equitable Transition.

I hold a PhD in Economics from Scuola Superiore Sant'Anna, a Master's degree in Economics and Economic Policy from the University of Bologna, and a Bachelor of Science in Economics from the University of Catania.

From May to September 2025, I was a Visiting Scholar in the Department of Economics at SOAS University of London.

My research interests are in macro-finance and financialization. Most of my projects are empirical and use long-run data, often informed by a political economy perspective. Another area of my research looks at how inequality and climate change are related.
